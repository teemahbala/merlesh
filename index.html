HOW TO MAKE RICE AND CHICKEN SAUCE

    Ingredients
  1. Boneless chicken
  2. Black pepper
  3. Chopped onions
  4. Corn flour
  5. Garlic powder
  6. Vegetable/coconut oil
  7. Minced ginger
  8. Mushroom
  9. Soy sauce
  10. Carrot,green pepper and yellow pepper
    Procedure
  • Wash your boneless chicken,add your black pepper
  • Add your chicken seasoning and garlic powder
  • Add corn flour and marinate for an hour
  • Put your vegetable oil in your frying pan{you're stir frying on high heat throughout!}
  • Fry your marinated chicken
  • Fry it till brown all over
  • Add minced ginger and garlic
  • Fry for a minute
  • Add chopped onions
  • Fry for 2 minutes
  • Put your vegetable oil in a different pan
  • Add carrot,green pepper and yellow pepper
  • Fry for 3 minutes
  • Add mushroom
  • Fry for 3 minutes
  • Add soy sauce and your fried chicken
  • Simmer for 2 minutes


    • ENJOY YOUR MEAL

HOW TO MAKE COCUMBER AND PINEAPPLE JUICE

    Procedure
  • Wash your cocumber and pineapple
  • Cut them into cubes
  • Put into your blender
  • Add water and blend it
  • After blending them pu them into a sieve
  • Place the sieve over a bowl to catch the juice
  • Add sugar or honey with ice cubes
